Get Engaged

Are you an individual interested in leading/facilitating discussions at TDLS?

Please send us an email or tweet at us with any poential topics that you want to cover

Are you an individual with ideas about how to make this site better?

Please go ahead to our GitHub repos and do any modifications you like through a Pull Request, or send us an email or tweet at us with your suggestion

Do you want to tell us what content/papers to cover?

Please send us an email or tweet at us with your favorite paper and we put it on our list

Do you want to hear about our videos as soon as they come out?

Please make sure you subscribe to our YouTube channel

Do you want to see our verdict on each paper we review?

Please make sure you follow us on Reddit and share your thoguhts with us

Do you want to own a new stream at TDLS?

Please check out the details of what it takes and write to us with your ideas

Are you a meetup organizer and want to work with us?

Great news! we want to work with you too! Let us know what you are thinking. We have access to lots of great folks who can speak about technical topics; check them out on our YouTube channel

What does it take to own a stream?

  • you define the stream including (why it should exist, what it includes, how it is done, who the target audience is, who the discussion panel can be, if the session format needs to be any different than typical, cadence, etc)
  • you coordinate with the steering committee members to make sure that there's venue sorted out but you're not responsible to secure venue
  • you coordinate with the steering committee members to ensure that the recording is sorted out but you're not responsible to secure that
  • you coordinate with discussion leads and facilitators for your sessions
  • you coordinate with the discussion panel about the paper they will present
  • you're responsible for communications with the panel members (initial set up, 2 weeks before, and post event, follow up if needed for the panel to send their slides in time for review and then the final version)
  • you moderate the sessions in your stream

About Us

Welcoming, supportive community of machine learning practitioner and researchers, coming from industry and academia, with an array of experience, from avid learners to experts in their fields. Our group is centred around consistent meetings focused on meticulous but friendly discussions of advances in machine learning (typically scientific publications), covering both intuitive understanding and foundational mathematical details.
