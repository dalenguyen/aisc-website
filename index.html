Toronto Deep Learning Series (TDLS)

Community of intellectually curious individuals centered around technical review and discussion of advances in machine learning.

Welcoming, supportive community of machine learning practitioner and researchers, coming from industry and academia, with an array of experience, from avid learners to experts in their fields.

Our group is centred around consistent meetings focused on meticulous but friendly discussions of advances in machine learning (typically scientific publications), covering both intuitive understanding and foundational mathematical details.
